What is CLS?

NSU CLS is North South University - Complain Lodging System where a student of North south university can complain about any kind of issues he or she face like harresment , cyber bullying .

Frequently Asked Questions

You must be a student or employee at North South University to sign up. You can click on the button 'Sign Up' at the top. Then fill out the information that are required to sign up.

You have to sign up at first to login. Then you can click on the button 'Login' at the top, and give your credentials to login.

You have to login at first to lodge a complaint. You can then click on the 'Add Complaint' button at the top and fill the required information to lodge a complaint.
